Our Story

Passion for Collecting,
Built for Collectors

Hoko Collectables was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate collectors who were frustrated with the lack of authentic, fairly-priced rare items online. We built the platform we wished existed.

Every item in our catalogue is hand-verified by our team of experts before listing. We work directly with reputable suppliers, private collectors, and estate sales to source genuine pieces you won't find anywhere else.
