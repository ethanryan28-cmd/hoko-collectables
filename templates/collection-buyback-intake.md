# HOKO Collectables — Collection Buyback Intake Email

Copy-paste this every time someone DMs / emails asking about selling a collection. Only customise the `[Name]` line — everything else stays the same. Consistency signals professionalism, and per PokeNE: sellers of valuable collections are *more* comfortable with rigid rules, not less.

---

**Subject:** HOKO Collectables — Collection Buyback Inquiry

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out about selling your collection. HOKO works a specific way so you know exactly what to expect — here's the full process upfront.

**What we buy:**
- Factory-sealed TCG product — Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, One Piece, Dragon Ball Super, and Japanese sealed
- Boxes, ETBs, booster packs, decks, promos, sealed accessories
- Graded slabs from PSA, BGS, or CGC — modern and vintage, **valued at AUD $100+ each**

**What we don't buy:**
- Loose singles (any value, any condition)
- Binders, bulk commons, playsets
- Damaged sealed product — torn seals, water damage, crushed boxes
- Graded slabs valued under AUD $100 each
- Slabs from grading services we can't verify (HGA, GMG, etc. — happy to clarify on request)

**Our offer:**
- We pay **75–80% of current fair market value**, based on TCGPlayer + eBay sold comps
- Quotes are valid for **48 hours** from when we send them
- Payment is **bank transfer on the day of pickup** — no PayPal, no cash, no instalments

**To get a quote, send us:**
1. A photo of the full collection laid out (one wide shot is fine — we'll ask for more if needed)
2. A typed list with: product name, set, language, quantity, condition (factory-sealed / opened / graded)
3. Your suburb / state for meet logistics

**Where we meet:**
- A local bank branch during business hours, OR
- A nominated storage facility during business hours
- No home pickups, no after-hours meets — this protects both sides.

If your collection includes loose singles or items we don't buy, we can usually point you to other reputable Aussie buyers, but HOKO itself is strictly sealed + graded.

Send the photo and list and I'll have a quote back within 48 hours.

## Usage notes

- **Voice/tone:** stern but warm. Don't soften the rules — that's the signal.
- **If they push back on a rule:** politely re-state it. Don't bend on meet location or payment method. Bending here is the "nice guy" trap that signals hobbyist.
- **If they have singles:** offer the referral, don't try to broker them. Stay in scope.
- **If they ask why so strict:** "We've been burned. The rules protect both sides — and they're the same for every seller, regardless of collection size."
- **48-hour quote turnaround** is a brand promise on hokocollectables.com — hit it or update the site.
