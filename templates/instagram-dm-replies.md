# Instagram / TikTok DM Reply Templates

Quick-copy reply templates for the most common DMs. Customise the [bracketed] parts. Tone: friendly, direct, collector-to-collector — never corporate, never pushy.

**Rule:** Never post on owner's behalf. These are drafts — copy, adjust if needed, send yourself.

---

## 1. Price check / "how much is this?"

**If it's listed on Shopify/eBay:**
> Hey! That one's listed at $[X] on the store — hokocollectables.com. Happy to answer any questions about it. Use HOKO10 for 10% off your first order 👊

**If it's not currently listed:**
> Hey! That one's not on the store right now but I can check what I've got. What condition / grade are you after? [OR: I don't have that one in stock at the moment but worth checking back — I'll tag you if it comes in.]

---

## 2. "Do you ship internationally?"

> Hey! We ship within Australia only at the moment — Express Post on all orders, free for orders over $100. If you're in NZ or elsewhere, flick me a message and we can chat but it's not something we do regularly yet.

---

## 3. "Can you hold this?" / "Can you reserve it?"

> Hey! We can't hold items without payment unfortunately — too many no-shows 😅. If you want to lock it in, easiest is to grab it on the store and I'll get it out same day. hokocollectables.com — HOKO10 for 10% off first order if you haven't used it.

---

## 4. "Is this still available?"

> Hey! [Yes — still available, listed at hokocollectables.com / No — that one's gone, sorry! I'll have more [product type] coming in — worth following to stay posted.]

---

## 5. "Can you do a deal?" / "Best price?"

> Hey! Prices are set at market — I don't really negotiate on individual items because I price them fair from the start. If you're buying a few things I'm always open to a chat though. What are you looking at? 👍

---

## 6. Buyback / "I want to sell my collection"

> Hey! Yeah we do buybacks on sealed TCG and PSA/BGS/CGC graded slabs. To get things started, I'll send through our intake form — it just asks for a photo of the collection and a list of what you've got. We quote within 48 hours from there. Want me to send it through?

*(If yes, send the collection-buyback-intake.md email template)*

---

## 7. "Do you buy singles?" / "Do you buy [HGA/ungraded] slabs?"

> Hey! We're focused on sealed TCG product and PSA/BGS/CGC graded slabs only at the moment — not buying loose singles or other grader slabs unfortunately. If you've got sealed or verified-grader slabs though, happy to take a look 👊

---

## 8. "When does [product] restock?"

> Hey! [Set name] is [in stock now / sold out at the moment]. I restock as I get supply in — best way to stay updated is to follow @hokocollectables and check the store. I post when new stock lands. hokocollectables.com

---

## 9. "Are your cards/products authentic?"

> Hey! Everything sealed is factory-sealed sourced through our supplier network. All slabs are PSA, BGS, or CGC only — we don't deal in other graders. Cert numbers are on every listing so you can verify directly with the grader. Any specific questions, happy to answer 👍

---

## 10. Complaint / bad experience

> Hey [name], really sorry to hear that. Can you DM me your order details and I'll sort it out — I want to make it right. [Do not argue, do not get defensive. Escalate to owner if needed.]

---

## 11. "Can I pick up?" / "Are you local?"

> Hey! We're Melbourne-based but we're online-only for sales — no pickup unfortunately. Everything ships Express Post, free over $100, same-day dispatch. hokocollectables.com

---

## 12. General collab / sponsorship request

> Hey! Thanks for reaching out — we're pretty small and selective about collabs at this stage. What did you have in mind? Happy to hear it.

*(Don't commit to anything, don't give discount codes to anyone without owner approval)*

---

## Tone reminders

- AU English — "yeah", "happy to", "👊", casual but professional
- Never say "unfortunately" twice in one message
- Never use "Hi there" — always "Hey" or "Hey [name]"
- Keep it short — 1-3 sentences max for most replies
- Never promise things you can't deliver (stock availability, hold times, prices)
- Buyback enquiries: always redirect to the formal intake process — never negotiate price in DMs
