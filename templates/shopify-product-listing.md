## How to use

1. Copy the relevant template below (Booster Box, ETB, or Slab).

## Copywriting rules

- AU English: favour, colour, organised, authorised.
- Prices in AUD. Never list in USD.
- Do not claim direct from Japan unless owner has confirmed for that specific SKU.
- Do not make investment or value claims.
- Do not call anything mint unless it is a graded slab with a verified grade.
- Always disclose condition notes -- honest grading is the moat.
- Voice: collector talking to another collector.
- Use we and our. Not HOKO in every sentence.
