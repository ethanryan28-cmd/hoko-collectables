## Terms

**1. What HOKO will do**
HOKO will list the item on its active sales channels (Shopify, eBay, Whatnot) at the agreed list price. HOKO may adjust pricing with consignor approval if market conditions change materially (>10% movement in comparable sold comps).

**2. Commission**
HOKO retains 20% of the final sale price (inclusive of any platform fees). Consignor receives 80% of the final sale price. If the item sells below agreed list price (e.g. Best Offer), HOKO will confirm with consignor before accepting.

**3. Payout**
Bank transfer within 5 business days of sale clearing. No PayPal, no cash, no instalments. Consignor provides BSB and account number at signing.

**4. Term**
This agreement is valid for **90 days** from signing. If unsold after 90 days, consignor may: (a) collect the item, or (b) extend the agreement in writing (email is sufficient).

**5. Scope**
HOKO only accepts consignment of: factory-sealed TCG product (Pokémon, MTG, One Piece, Dragon Ball Super) and PSA / BGS / CGC graded slabs valued at AUD $100 or more. No loose singles, no unverified-grader slabs, no damaged sealed.

**6. Care & custody**
HOKO will store the item securely and pack it appropriately for sale. HOKO is not liable for loss or damage beyond its reasonable control (e.g. carrier damage after handover). Consignor is encouraged to photograph the item at handover.

**7. Return**
If the item is unsold at agreement end, HOKO will return it at consignor's cost (or consignor may collect in person). Condition at return will match condition at intake.

**8. Termination**
Either party may terminate this agreement with 48 hours written notice (email). If HOKO terminates, item is returned at HOKO's cost. If consignor terminates, item is returned at consignor's cost.

## Usage notes (for Ethan — remove before printing)

- **When to use:** A seller contacts you about a collection that fits sealed + slabs and wants top dollar rather than an immediate buyback. Offer consignment as the alternative ("cash now at 75–80%, or wait and get ~80% via consignment").
- **Don't market consignment yet** — casual cases only. Formal Phase 2 launch (intake form, tracking sheet, marketing) waits until Whatnot + Shopify flow is proven.
- **One agreement per item** — not per collection. If a seller has 3 slabs, print 3 agreements.
- **Photo at handover** — always photograph the item together before handing it off. Date the photo.
- **Tracking:** Log each consignment in a spreadsheet: Consignor / Item / Cert # / List Price / Date In / Date Sold / Sale Price / Commission / Payout Status.
- **Commission rate:** This template uses flat 20%. Review at Phase 2 formal launch whether to move to a sliding scale by item value.
- **If best offer comes in below list:** Always confirm with consignor before accepting. This is their item — they have final say on price.
