# Templates

Ready-to-use document templates for HOKO Collectables. Each file is a structured starting point — fill in the bracketed fields, review, then send or publish.

---

## Template index

| File | Purpose | When to use |
|------|---------|-------------|
| `shopify-product-listing.md` | Full product listing template for Shopify | Every new sealed product or slab added to the store |
| `ebay-product-listing.md` | eBay listing template with condition notes and postage | Every new eBay listing |
| `consignment-agreement.md` | Formal consignment agreement document | When accepting Phase 2 consignment stock |
| `consignment-intake-email.md` | Email to send consignor after receiving stock | After consignment stock arrives and is logged |
| `collection-buyback-intake.md` | Buyback intake form / quote summary | After completing a buyback assessment |
| `instagram-dm-replies.md` | Quick-copy DM reply bank for common Instagram enquiries | When replying to Instagram DMs |

---

## Usage notes

- All templates use `[SQUARE BRACKETS]` for fields you need to fill in before sending.
- Templates are starting points — always read through before sending and adjust tone or detail as needed.
- For listing templates, check the pricing rules in `routines/prompts/pricing-update.md` before publishing.
- Consignment templates are **Phase 2 only** — do not actively market consignment yet.
- For DM replies, tone reminders are embedded at the bottom of `instagram-dm-replies.md`.
