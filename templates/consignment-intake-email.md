# HOKO Collectables — Consignment Intake Email

Copy-paste this every time a seller DMs or emails asking about consigning. Only customise the `[Name]` line — everything else stays the same. Mirror the buyback intake tone: stern, professional, copy-paste. Consistency signals legitimate operation.

---

**Subject:** HOKO Collectables — Consignment Inquiry

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out. HOKO offers two options for sellers — here's how consignment works and what we accept.

**What consignment means:**
Instead of a cash buyback, HOKO lists your item on our channels (Shopify, eBay, Whatnot) and takes a **20% commission** when it sells. You receive **80% of the sale price**, paid by bank transfer after the sale clears. No upfront cash — you wait for the sale, but you keep more of the value.

If you want cash today instead, our buyback offer is **75–80% of fair market value**, paid on pickup day.

**What we accept for consignment:**
- Factory-sealed TCG product — Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, One Piece, Dragon Ball Super (English or Japanese)
- Graded slabs from **PSA, BGS, or CGC only** — valued at **AUD $100+ each**

**What we don't accept:**
- Loose singles, binders, bulk
- Damaged or opened sealed product
- Graded slabs under AUD $100 each
- Slabs from grading services we can't verify (HGA, GMG, etc.)

**To get a consignment quote, send us:**
1. A photo of the item (one clear shot is fine)
2. For graded slabs: cert number and grade
3. For sealed product: set name, language, quantity, condition
4. Your suburb/state

**How it works:**
1. We agree on a list price
2. You sign a one-page consignment agreement (we'll send the form)
3. We meet at a bank branch or storage facility during business hours to hand over the item
4. We list it across our channels
5. When it sells, we transfer your payout (80% of final sale price) within 5 business days

**Timing:**
Consignment agreements are for **90 days**. If unsold after 90 days, you can collect the item or extend in writing.

Send the photo and details and I'll come back to you with a proposed list price and next steps.

## Usage notes

- **Tone:** Same as buyback — stern but warm. Copy-paste every time. Don't soften the rules.
- **Buyback vs consignment pitch:** "Cash now at 75–80% (buyback), or wait and get ~80% via consignment — your call." Let them choose. Don't push one over the other.
- **Don't market this yet** — Phase 2 formal launch only. Use this template for inbound sellers only.
- **If they push on commission rate:** 20% is firm. Covers listing time, platform fees, and storage. Sellers getting 80% of FMV on consignment vs 75–80% upfront on buyback — consignment is already the better financial deal for them.
- **Agreement:** Once agreed in principle, send `templates/consignment-agreement.md`. One form per item.
- **Meet location:** Same as buyback — bank branch or storage facility, business hours only. No home pickups, no after-hours.
- **Photo at handover:** Always. Date the photo. This protects both sides.
