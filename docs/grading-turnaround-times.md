# Grading Turnaround Times

Reference guide for PSA, BGS, and CGC grading turnaround times and service tiers.
Times are approximate — always check the grader's current website before submitting.
Turnaround times change frequently, especially during peak periods.

Last updated: June 2026 (verify current times at psa.com / beckett.com / cgccards.com)

---

## PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator)

| Service Level | Target Turnaround | Price per Card (approx USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy | 40-60 business days | $18 |
| Regular | 20 business days | $25 |
| Express | 10 business days | $50 |
| Super Express | 5 business days | $100 |
| Walkthrough | 2 business days | $300 |

**Notes:**
- PSA is the most recognised grader for Pokemon and sports cards
- PSA 10 commands the highest premiums in the market
- Economy tier recommended for bulk submissions on common cards
- Use Express/Super Express only if card value justifies it
- Domestic AU submissions require a PSA-authorised dealer or direct membership

---

## BGS / Beckett

| Service Level | Target Turnaround | Price per Card (approx USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy | 30-40 business days | $18 |
| Standard | 15-20 business days | $22 |
| Express | 10 business days | $45 |
| Super Express | 5 business days | $85 |
| Pristine | Variable | $150+ |

**Notes:**
- BGS uses sub-grades (centering, corners, edges, surface) — more detail than PSA
- BGS 9.5 (Gem Mint) is highly sought after, especially in Pokemon
- BGS 10 Pristine is extremely rare and commands large premiums
- Good for cards where surface condition is a key selling point

---

## CGC (Certified Guaranty Company)

| Service Level | Target Turnaround | Price per Card (approx USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy | 45-60 business days | $12 |
| Standard | 20 business days | $18 |
| Express | 10 business days | $40 |
| Walkthrough | 3 business days | $200 |

**Notes:**
- CGC originally known for comics — expanding strongly into TCG
- CGC graded Pokemon and MTG cards growing in acceptance
- Good budget option for mid-range cards
- CGC 10 Pristine designation for flawless cards

## Submission Viability Check

Before submitting, use `routines/prompts/grading-submission.md` to assess whether a card
is worth grading at each tier.

Quick rule: submission cost should not exceed 15-20% of expected graded card value.

Example: Card expected to grade PSA 10 at $200
- Max spend on grading: $30-40
- Economy ($18) is justified
- Express ($50) is borderline — only if time is critical

## Important Notes

- Turnaround times are targets — not guarantees
- Check psa.com/orderqueue for current wait times before submitting
- Factor in shipping time to/from the US for international submissions
- Use a PSA-authorised AU dealer to ship domestically where possible
- Always insure grading submissions in transit (see `docs/insurance-guide.md`)
