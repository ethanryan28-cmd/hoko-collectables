# Content Calendar

Social media content planning framework for HOKO Collectables.
Use this to plan weekly and monthly content across TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

---

## Weekly Content Targets

| Platform | Target per week | Types |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 3-5 videos | Product shows, openings, market updates, educational |
| Instagram | 4-6 posts/stories | Product photos, reposts of TikTok, behind the scenes |
| Facebook | 2-3 posts | Product announcements, market news, event notices |

Minimum viable week: 1 TikTok + 2 Instagram posts.

---

## Content Types by Priority

**Tier 1 — Always works:**
- New arrival product show (slab or sealed)
- Pack opening / reveal
- Graded card show (PSA 10, BGS 9.5)

**Tier 2 — Strong engagement:**
- Market update (when there is genuine news)
- Behind the scenes (packing, sourcing, grading)
- Educational content (how grading works, how to buy safely)

**Tier 3 — Supplementary:**
- Convention/event updates
- Buyback process walkthrough
- Collector tips

---

## Monthly Content Schedule

| Week | Focus | Prompts to Use |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | New arrivals from last month | `product-launch.md` |
| Week 2 | Market update + educational | `market-update.md`, original content |
| Week 3 | Behind the scenes + buyback | Original content |
| Week 4 | Convention/event + preview | `convention-post-content.md` if applicable |
| End of month | Newsletter + monthly recap | `email-newsletter.md` |

---

## Content Rules

**Always:**
- AU English
- Price transparency — include price if listing the item
- No fake urgency or scarcity language
- Tag the platform link (link in bio for Instagram, direct for TikTok)

**Never:**
- Post on behalf of Ethan without his approval
- Fabricate card names, prices, or sold comparisons
- Mention consignment — Phase 2 only
- Use HOKO10 code in public posts
- Make financial advice claims

**Core tags (use on most posts):**

**Location tags:**
#melbournecollector #australiatcg #pokemonaustralia

#bgs #cgc #psa10 #onepiecetcg #dragonballtcg #sealedtcg

## Posting Times (AEST)

**Best performing windows:**
- TikTok: 7-9pm weekdays, 12-2pm weekends
- Instagram: 6-8pm weekdays
- Facebook: 12-2pm or 6-8pm

Schedule posts in advance when possible to maintain consistency.
