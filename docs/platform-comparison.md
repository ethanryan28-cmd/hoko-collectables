# Platform Comparison

Quick-reference comparison of HOKO's selling platforms.
Use this to decide where to list an item, price it, and how to handle fees.

---

## Platform Overview

| Platform | Best For | Audience | Fees | Reach |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| eBay AU | High-value slabs, sealed | Broad — local + international | ~13-14% (FVF + PayPal) | Highest reach |
| Shopify | All products, repeat buyers | Direct — drives own traffic | ~2-3% (payment) | Medium |
| Whatnot | Live sales, sealed product | Collector community | ~8% commission | Growing AU |

---

## eBay

**Best for:** PSA/BGS/CGC slabs, sealed product with strong comps, international buyers

**Fee breakdown (approximate):**
- Final Value Fee: ~13.4% (calculated on total including shipping)
- Promoted Listings (optional): 2-5% extra if used
- Payment processing: included in FVF

**Pros:**
- Largest buyer pool in Australia
- Best for one-off high-value items
- Buyer protection drives trust
- Best for international exposure

**Cons:**
- Highest fees
- Disputes handled by eBay (not always in seller's favour)
- Requires active listing management

**HOKO tips:**
- Always include cert number in listing title for PSA/BGS/CGC
- Use Best Offer on slabs $300+
- Keep eBay health above TRS threshold (see `ebay-reputation-guide.md`)

---

## Shopify

**Best for:** All products, building repeat buyers, newsletter marketing

**Fee breakdown (approximate):**
- Payment processing: ~1.75% + $0.30 per transaction (Shopify Payments AU)
- No per-listing or per-sale fees beyond payment processing
- Shopify plan fee: monthly subscription (~$39-$105 AUD/month)

**Pros:**
- Lowest per-sale fees
- Full control over branding, listings, and customer data
- Email list (Klaviyo) drives repeat sales
- No auction-style risk

**Cons:**
- Drives own traffic — requires SEO + social media
- Lower impulse buyer volume than eBay

**HOKO tips:**
- Mirror all eBay listings to Shopify
- Use Shopify as the home base for brand + newsletter
- Cross-link from social content to Shopify listings

---

## Whatnot

**Best for:** Sealed product, live shows, community building, moving multiple items quickly

**Fee breakdown:**
- Seller commission: 8% of sale price
- Shipping: buyer pays direct
- No listing fees

**Pros:**
- Live engagement drives impulse buys
- Great for sealed product that doesn't have strong static listing comps
- Builds community and repeat Whatnot followers

**Cons:**
- Requires time commitment (live shows)
- Less effective for high-value individual slabs (buyers prefer to inspect)
- Growing AU market but still smaller than eBay

**HOKO tips:**
- Use Whatnot for sealed product runs and bulk slab lots
- Don't sell $1000+ slabs on Whatnot — buyers prefer eBay/Shopify for that
- See `whatnot-setup-guide.md` for full setup and show tips

## Which Platform to Use

| Item Type | Primary Platform | Secondary |
|---|---|---|
| PSA/BGS/CGC slab $100-$500 | eBay + Shopify | — |
| PSA/BGS/CGC slab $500+ | eBay (Best Offer) + Shopify | — |
| Sealed ETB / booster box | eBay + Shopify + Whatnot | — |
| Sealed UPC / display | Shopify + Whatnot | eBay |
| Bulk sealed lots | Whatnot | eBay |

## Minimum Viable Price Reminder

Always calculate net return after fees before listing:

**eBay:** Price / 1.134 = net (before cost)
**Shopify:** Price / 1.03 = net (before cost)
**Whatnot:** Price / 1.08 = net (before cost)

Never list below replacement cost on any platform.
