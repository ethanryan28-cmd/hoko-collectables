## 2. Run-of-show (2-hour structure)

Use as scaffolding. Move blocks around to fit what you've got.

| Time | Segment | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **0:00 – 0:05** | Intro + why this stream exists | Frame the pivot — turns liquidation into brand story |
| **0:05 – 0:25** | Bulk lot blowouts (low entry price, $1-5 starts) | Builds room energy + buyer momentum early |
| **0:25 – 0:55** | Mid-tier singles ($20-100) — solo cards or small lots | Highest-volume sales window |
| **0:55 – 1:05** | **Mid-show break — feature ONE sealed product** | Plant the seed: "this is what's on Shopify now. Use HOKO10." |
| **1:05 – 1:35** | Premium singles ($100+) — slabs, chase cards | Peak engagement window for higher-ticket items |
| **1:35 – 1:55** | Final clearance — "anything left, $X flat" | Clears tail inventory + leaves buyers feeling they got a deal |
| **1:55 – 2:00** | Outro — thank you, follow, hokocollectables.com | Channel the audience off-platform |

**Intro script (literal, 60 seconds):**
> "Hey, I'm Ethan from HOKO Collectables in Melbourne. Quick context before we start — I'm closing out my English singles collection tonight because HOKO is going sealed-only. Sealed product, graded slabs, that's the focus going forward. Everything raw, everything cheap, everything that's been sitting — it's all going tonight. Honest grading, careful packing, same-day dispatch. Let's get into it."

## 7. Physical prep checklist (do day-of)

**Inventory prep:**

- [ ] Pull all singles to be sold; sort into bins by price tier ($1-5 bulk / $5-20 mid / $20-100 premium / $100+ flagship)
- [ ] Slabs: one stack, sorted highest-to-lowest value
- [ ] Bulk lots: pre-bag in groups of 100 if going to do "100x lot blowouts"
- [ ] Top loaders + penny sleeves + bubble mailers stocked for dispatch tomorrow
- [ ] [FILL IN: 2-3 chase items pulled and within camera reach for the listing description]

**Tech / setup:**

- [ ] Phone on tripod or stand at table-level (not below — looks amateur)
- [ ] Lighting: one ring light or overhead lamp pointed at the card surface, no shadows
- [ ] Card surface: plain dark mat or felt — kills glare and frames the cards
- [ ] Phone charger plugged in (2-hour show drains battery)
- [ ] Pinned chat messages typed and ready to paste
- [ ] HOKO10 code visible — print a small sign and tape it just out of frame so you can glance at it
- [ ] Notepad for buyer usernames / shipping notes during the show

**Brand:**

- [ ] HOKO logo / banner visible somewhere in frame (printed sign or backdrop)
- [ ] Phone notifications on silent (no IG ping interruptions during high-bid moments)
- [ ] Glass of water within reach (you'll talk for 2 hours)
