Qty:4,816# Market Update — Social Post Prompt

Use this prompt to generate a short market update post for Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook.
Best used when there's genuine market news worth sharing — don't force it.

---

## When to Use

- A key card has jumped or dropped significantly in price
- A new set announcement affects the value of existing sealed product
- PSA/BGS/CGC has updated grading standards or turnaround times
- A major sale has happened (e.g. auction record for a specific card)
- Market conditions are affecting buyback volume

Do not post market updates as filler. Only post when there's something genuinely useful.

---

## Inputs to Gather Before Running

| Input | Example |
|---|---|
| What happened | PSA 10 Charizard Base Set sold for $15k USD at auction |
| Why it matters | Sets a new benchmark for raw card buyback valuation |
| Game / set affected | Pokemon — Base Set, 1st Edition |
| Who this affects | Sellers considering grading + buyers of slabs |
| HOKO's position | We hold 2 PSA 9s — values up, not currently selling |
| Platform | Instagram / TikTok / Facebook |
| Tone direction | Informative / Cautionary / Excited |

---

## The Prompt

```
You are writing a short market update post for HOKO Collectables' social media.
HOKO is a Melbourne-based graded slab and sealed TCG seller. Voice: collector-to-collector,
honest, AU English. Never hype, never pump.

Write a market update post based on the following:

What happened: [DESCRIBE THE MARKET EVENT]
Why it matters: [EXPLAIN THE SIGNIFICANCE]
Game/set affected: [GAME AND SET]
Who this affects: [BUYERS / SELLERS / COLLECTORS]
HOKO's position: [OPTIONAL — include only if relevant and verified]
Platform: [INSTAGRAM / TIKTOK / FACEBOOK]
Tone: [INFORMATIVE / CAUTIONARY / EXCITED]

Output:
1. POST CAPTION (150-200 words for Instagram, 100-150 for Facebook, TikTok script format if TikTok)
2. 3-5 HASHTAGS (relevant, not generic)
3. CTA (one line — optional, low pressure)
4. TIMING NOTE (is this time-sensitive? If so, post within X hours)

Rules:
- AU English throughout
- Never fabricate prices or sale results — only use what's in the inputs
- If HOKO holds relevant stock, include only if owner has confirmed it's accurate
- Don't advise people to buy or sell — just share the information
- No 'now is the time to buy/sell' language
- No urgency language or countdown language
```

---

## Example Use Case

**Event:** A BGS 10 Pikachu Illustrator sells for a record price
**Output:** Short post explaining what the card is, why it sold for that amount,
what it means for the broader vintage card market, and a low-key CTA to follow HOKO for more updates.

---

## What NOT to Post

- Speculation about future prices
- Price predictions ('this card will be worth X by end of year')
- Pump posts ('now's the time to buy before prices go higher')
- Negative posts about competitors' pricing
- Content that could be seen as financial advice

---

## Frequency

Post market updates maximum once per week — quality over quantity.
Only post when the event is genuinely notable and the information is accurate.
