# Milestone Announcement — Social Post Prompt

Use this prompt when HOKO hits a significant milestone worth sharing publicly.
Keep announcements genuine and specific — avoid vague celebrations.

---

## When to Use

- Hitting a feedback milestone on eBay (100, 250, 500 positive)
- First Whatnot show completed
- First full buyback completed
- Business anniversary (1 year, 2 year)
- Major stock milestone (first PSA 10 above $1k sold)
- Community milestone (1,000 followers, 500 customers)

Do not announce minor milestones or create artificial celebrations.

---

## Inputs to Gather Before Running

| Input | Example |
|---|---|
| Milestone achieved | 100 positive eBay feedback with zero negatives |
| What it means | Verified track record — 100 buyers got what they paid for |
| Date achieved | June 2026 |
| Platform | Instagram / TikTok / Facebook |
| Tone | Grateful, grounded — not self-congratulatory |

---

## The Prompt

```
Write a milestone announcement post for HOKO Collectables.
HOKO is a Melbourne-based graded slab and sealed TCG seller.
Voice: collector-to-collector, honest, AU English. No hype or empty self-promotion.

Milestone: [DESCRIBE IT]
What it means: [GENUINE SIGNIFICANCE — max 2 sentences]
Date: [MONTH YEAR]
Platform: [INSTAGRAM / TIKTOK / FACEBOOK]
Tone: [GRATEFUL / REFLECTIVE / EXCITED]

Output:
1. POST (100-200 words — personal, not corporate)
2. OPENING LINE (hook — stop the scroll without being clickbait)
3. 3 HASHTAGS (relevant to the milestone)
4. OPTIONAL CTA (follow for more, check the store — one only, soft)

Rules:
- Write as Ethan, not as a brand
- Be specific — vague milestone posts feel hollow
- Thank the community without being sycophantic
- No phrases like "humbled and grateful", "on this journey", "blessed"
- No giveaway announcements tied to milestones unless separately confirmed
- AU English
```

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do not manufacture a milestone (e.g. "50 orders this month!")
- Do not ask followers to "help us reach X" — growth should be organic
- Do not do a giveaway as part of the announcement without a separate plan
- Do not make the post sound corporate or PR-written

---
