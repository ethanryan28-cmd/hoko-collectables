# Prompt: Convention / Card Show Prep

Use when: you're heading to a card show, convention, or market. Run this 3-5 days before the event to generate a full run sheet.

---

## Inputs (fill in before running)

```
Event name: [e.g. Sydney TCG Expo, local card show]
Event date: [DD/MM/YYYY]
Event location: [suburb/venue]
Table size: [e.g. 6ft trestle, 1.8m table]
Setup time: [e.g. 7:30am]
Duration: [e.g. 9am-4pm]
Expected crowd: [casual/enthusiast/competitive]
What I'm selling: [brief list — e.g. sealed booster boxes, graded slabs, ETBs]
Price range of items: [e.g. $20-$800]
Cash float needed: [e.g. $200 in small notes and coins]
EFTPOS available?: [yes — Square / yes — Tap to Pay on iPhone / no]
```

---

## What to produce

### 1. Packing checklist

**Product:**
- [ ] Inventory list (printed or on phone — what you're bringing + prices)
- [ ] All sealed product (double-check quantities against inventory list)
- [ ] All graded slabs in secure slab case or padded carry case
- [ ] Toploader display sleeves for any slabs on display (so the slab itself isn't handled)
- [ ] Pricing labels / stickers on every item or clearly visible price tag

**Display:**
- [ ] Table cover / cloth (black or branded)
- [ ] Display risers or stands (tiered display looks more professional)
- [ ] Slab stands or acrylic display stands for hero slabs
- [ ] HOKO Collectables signage / banner if available
- [ ] Price display cards for tiered/bulk pricing if applicable

**Payments:**
- [ ] Square reader (charged)
- [ ] Phone with Tap to Pay enabled and tested
- [ ] Cash float: $[X] — suggest $50 in $5s, $100 in $10s, $50 in $20s, coins for $1/$2
- [ ] Receipt book or ability to send digital receipts

**Admin:**
- [ ] Business cards or HOKO10 discount cards (give one with every purchase)
- [ ] Bags/packaging for sold items (bubble wrap for slabs, bags for sealed)
- [ ] Packing tape + scissors
- [ ] Phone charger / portable battery
- [ ] Water + food (don't rely on venue)
- [ ] COVID/hygiene hand sanitiser

**Security:**
- [ ] Lock box or bum bag for cash
- [ ] Don't leave table unattended — have a plan if you need a break
- [ ] High-value slabs ($200+): keep in carry case behind table, bring out to show only

---

### 2. Pricing strategy for the day

Based on the event profile ([expected crowd]):
- **Casual crowd:** price at market; be ready to explain grading (many won't know PSA vs raw)
- **Enthusiast crowd:** price at market, no discounts needed — they know value
- **Competitive/trader crowd:** price firm; if you bundle-deal, set a floor (e.g. "buy 3 sealed ETBs, $X each")

Bundle offer to have ready:
> "Buy 2 or more sealed boxes, I'll throw in free shipping if you want anything from the online store too — here's my card."

Do NOT undercut your Shopify/eBay prices to move stock — your online buyers will notice. Convention pricing = at market, same as online.

---

### 3. Elevator pitch (memorise or save to phone)

> "HOKO Collectables — Melbourne-based, collector-run. We do sealed TCG and PSA/BGS/CGC graded slabs. Everything's priced at market, honest grading, same-day dispatch on online orders. If you're looking to sell a collection, we do buybacks too — here's my card."

---

### 4. Post-show to-do list

Generate this section as a checklist:
- [ ] Count remaining inventory vs what you brought (know what sold)
- [ ] Count cash + reconcile with Square/Tap to Pay totals
- [ ] Record sales in a note or spreadsheet: items sold, price, channel (cash/card)
- [ ] Any buyback enquiries from the day? Follow up within 48 hours
- [ ] Restock trigger: flag any SKU that sold out or dropped below 2 units
- [ ] Content from the day: did you get any photos/video? Schedule posts this week
- [ ] Update eBay/Shopify quantities if any display stock was sold

---

**Day before:**
> "Heading to [event name] this [day] in [suburb]! Come find us — we'll have sealed [games] and graded slabs on the table. Table [number if known]. Collector first. Seller second. #hokocollectables #[game]tcg #melbourne"

**Morning of (story):**
> "Setting up at [event name] — table ready. Come say hi if you're here today 🤙 [photo of table setup]"

**Post-show:**
> "[Recap post — pulls of the day, interesting sales, what sold out, buyback conversations]"

---

## Reminders (never skip)

- Give a HOKO10 card to every buyer — this is the conversion from convention sale to Shopify repeat customer
- Buyback at conventions: take their contact details and inventory photo, send formal quote within 48 hours via the intake email template — don't negotiate on the spot
- Never accept a buyback for HGA/GMG/unverified grader slabs — politely decline on the day
- Pack remaining inventory securely before leaving — don't rush the pack-down
