# TikTok / Reels Content Prompt

**Trigger:** When filming or planning short-form video content for TikTok or Instagram Reels.
**Use for:** Generating video hooks, scripts, caption text, and hashtag sets for short-form video.

---

## Inputs

Before generating content, confirm:

- **Video type:** [choose from list below]
- **Product featured:** [product name, grade/cert if slab, or 'no specific product']
- **Key fact or hook moment:** [e.g. 'this card sold for $X', 'I paid $X at a show', 'look at this quad 10']
- **Platform:** TikTok / Instagram Reels / Both
- **Target length:** [15 sec / 30 sec / 60 sec]

---

## Video types

Choose one:

| Type | Format |
|------|--------|
| Slab reveal | Hold card face-down, flip to camera |
| Pack opening | Open sealed product on camera |
| Grading result | Show cert number being looked up + result reveal |
| New stock haul | Spread new arrivals on table, show each one |
| Price check | Show current market price on screen + comment |
| Card story | Brief history or context about a specific card or set |
| Show recap | Highlights from a Whatnot show or card fair |
| Day in the life | Behind-the-scenes of running HOKO |
| Hot take | Opinion piece — e.g. 'you're buying the wrong slabs' |
| Tutorial | How to check a PSA cert / how to grade your cards |

---

## Outputs to generate

### 1. Hook (first 3 seconds)
Generate 3 hook options for the chosen video type. Hooks must:
- Start with something that creates pattern interruption or curiosity
- Be under 10 words for on-screen text
- Be natural when spoken aloud (not corporate or cringey)

Examples by type:
- Reveal: "I've been sitting on this for 6 months"
- Pack opening: "These are going for $X on eBay right now"
- Grading result: "We sent this expecting a 7. Got something better."
- Hot take: "Stop buying PSA 9s. Here's why."

### 2. Script / talking points
A brief point-form script for the video. Should include:
- What to say in each section (intro, middle, close)
- Any key facts or numbers to mention
- Call to action at the end (follow, DM for price, link in bio)

Keep it natural — Ethan speaks like a collector, not a salesperson.

### 3. Caption (for post)
- 2-4 sentences max
- Include the key hook or surprising fact
- End with a question or CTA to drive comments
- Do NOT use excessive emojis — 1-2 max
- Do NOT use marketing buzzwords

### 4. Hashtag set
Generate two sets:

**TikTok (20-25 tags):**
Mix of: niche-specific (PokemonCards, GradedCards, PSA), mid-size (TCG, Collectables), broad (CardCollector, Australia)

**Instagram Reels (10-15 tags):**
Fewer, more targeted — focus on AU collector community tags

### 5. Thumbnail / cover frame suggestion
Describe the ideal static image for the video cover:
- What's in frame
- Any on-screen text (keep to 3-5 words max)
- Where the card/product should be positioned

---

## Tone notes

- Voice: casual, collector-to-collector — like you're talking to a mate who also collects
- No hype language: never say 'INSANE', 'CRAZY PULL', 'YOU WON'T BELIEVE' unless it's genuinely earned
- Humour is fine — dry, self-aware, low-key
- If talking about prices: be accurate, not sensational
- Never make claims about future value or investment potential

---

## Content calendar integration

Aim for 3-4 short-form videos per week across TikTok and Reels.
Suggested mix:

| Day | Content type |
|-----|-------------|
| Mon | Product / slab showcase |
| Wed | Educational or hot take |
| Fri | Pull, reveal, or haul |
| Sun | Show recap or behind-the-scenes |

Adjust based on what's happening in the shop — always prioritise timely content (fresh pulls, new arrivals) over planned content.
