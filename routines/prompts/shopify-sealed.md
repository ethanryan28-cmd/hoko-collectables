# Shopify Product Page — Sealed Product

Generate a Shopify product page for a sealed TCG product.

## Inputs

- **Product name:**
- **Set / expansion:**
- **Format:** Booster Box / ETB / Bundle / Collection Box / Tin / Booster Pack / Other
- **Region:** English / Japanese / Korean / Chinese / Other
- **Release date:**
- **In stock / pre-order:**
- **Price (AUD):**
- **Notable chase cards in set:**

## Output

- **Title** — clean Shopify product title, no SEO keyword spam
- **Meta description** — ~155 chars for Google
- **Product description** — 3 short paragraphs:
  1. The set + why collectors and players want it right now
  2. What's inside (pack count, card count, guaranteed slots, art rarities)
  3. Chase cards by name + why they matter (facts, not hype)
- **Bullet block** at end: dispatch (same-day if before 2pm AEST), packaging (boxed, padded), HOKO trust signals
- **Suggested tags** for Shopify (set, format, region, year, language)
- **Suggested collection assignments** (e.g. "Pokemon Sealed", "Pre-orders", "Japanese", "Booster Boxes")

Voice: knowledgeable collector explaining to another collector why this is worth the money. Never "Get yours now!" hype.
