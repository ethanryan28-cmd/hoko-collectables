# Buyer Follow-Up Prompt

**Trigger:** When a buyer has made 2+ purchases, or when Ethan wants to personally reach out to a valued repeat customer.
**Use for:** Building genuine collector relationships — not bulk marketing. These are personal messages.

---

## Inputs

- **Buyer username/name:** [eBay username or Shopify name]
- **Platform:** eBay / Shopify / Whatnot / Instagram
- **What they bought (most recent):** [product name]
- **What they bought previously:** [previous purchase(s)]
- **Any known collecting interest?** [e.g. 'vintage Pokemon', 'BGS 10s', 'MTG Commander'] or 'unknown'
- **Reason for reaching out:** [choose one below]
  - 2nd+ purchase milestone
  - You just got new stock relevant to their collection
  - Post-show follow-up (they attended a Whatnot show)
  - Buyback outreach (they may have cards they'd sell)
  - General check-in

---

## Message templates

### Repeat buyer — 2nd purchase milestone
```
Hey [NAME],

Just packed up your [ITEM] — should be with you in a day or two.

Noticed this is your second order with us — genuinely appreciate the support. If you're ever looking for something specific that's not listed, feel free to DM and I'll keep an eye out.

Cheers,
Ethan — HOKO Collectables
```

### Relevant new stock just arrived
```
Hey [NAME],

Quick one — just got [ITEM/STOCK DESCRIPTION] in and thought of you based on your previous [PURCHASE].

Haven't listed it yet but happy to send photos if you're interested.

No pressure — just thought it was worth a heads-up.

Cheers,
Ethan — HOKO Collectables
```

### Post-Whatnot show follow-up
```
Hey [NAME],

Thanks for joining the show last [DAY] — good to have you in there.

Hope the [ITEM THEY WON] arrived safely. Got another show coming up on [DATE] if you want to jump in — should have [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF WHAT'S COMING].

Cheers,
Ethan — HOKO Collectables
```

### Buyback outreach (soft approach)
```
Hey [NAME],

Hope the [LAST PURCHASE] is treating you well.

Quick question — we're actively buying PSA/BGS/CGC slabs at the moment if you ever have anything you're looking to move on. No pressure at all, just thought I'd mention it since you know how we operate.

If there's ever anything you're thinking about selling, feel free to reach out and I can put together a quote.

Cheers,
Ethan — HOKO Collectables
```

### General check-in (long-term buyer)
```
Hey [NAME],

Just wanted to reach out — it's been a while since your last order and I wasn't sure if there was anything you were chasing.

We've had some good [CATEGORY] stock come through lately if that's still your thing. Happy to send you what we've got.

Cheers,
Ethan — HOKO Collectables
```

---

## Tone notes

- Keep every message short — 4-6 lines max
- Always personalise with the specific item they bought — no generic messages
- Never pitch multiple things in one message
- The goal is a conversation, not a sale — don't close hard
- If they don't reply, don't follow up again for at least 30 days
- Only reach out to buyers who've actually purchased — never cold message
- Buyback outreach: only use the soft template — never pressure or negotiate via DM

---

## Who to prioritise

Reach out proactively to buyers who:
- Have bought 3+ times
- Spent over $300 in total
- Left positive feedback mentioning specific cards or sets (they care)
- Engaged with your Whatnot shows regularly
