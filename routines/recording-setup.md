# Recording / streaming setup — HOKO Collectables

Single physical setup that serves THREE purposes:

1. **Product photos** for Shopify listings (slabs, sealed)
2. **Slab Reveal videos** for TikTok / IG Reels
3. **Whatnot live streams** (sealed pack rips, singles liquidation)

Build it once. Use it for everything.

---

```
              [Overhead phone clamp]

              [Phone — overhead shot]

                       ▼
              ┌────────────────────┐
              │   DARK PLAYMAT     │  ← cards / slabs here

                            │

Power: USB cable to wall, phone never below 50%
```

## Bare minimum — start tonight ($0-50)

Don't let perfect be the enemy of running. You can stream Whatnot tonight with:

- [ ] Your phone (Whatnot streams from the app — that's the camera)
- [ ] A stack of books / box to prop the phone overhead
- [ ] Window light during the day OR any desk lamp angled at the cards
- [ ] Any flat dark surface — folded dark t-shirt, black cardstock, even a binder cover
- [ ] Phone plugged into a charger (2-hour streams drain battery)
- [ ] Glass of water

Run a 30-min test stream privately before your first real show. Verify camera angle, audio level, lighting.

## Recommended setup — do it right (~$200-400 AUD total)

Buy in stages. Audio first, then lighting, then mounts.

### Tier 1 — Audio (do this first, ~$80-280)

**This is the most underrated investment.** Whatnot buyers tolerate okay video but bail on bad audio. A lav mic is the single best gear upgrade you can make.

| Option | Cost AUD | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Boya BY-WM4 Pro** | ~$70-90 | Entry-level wireless. Works but Bluetooth latency can be iffy. |
| **RØDE Wireless ME** | ~$180-220 | **Sweet spot.** Pro audio quality, simple, reliable. Best value. |
| **RØDE Wireless GO II** | ~$350-400 | Dual receiver if you ever co-host. Overkill for solo streams. |
| **DJI Mic** | ~$280-330 | Slightly fancier than the ME, similar quality. Good if you also use DJI gear. |

**Recommendation:** RØDE Wireless ME at ~$200. Pro tier, no faff. Phone-compatible out of the box (Lightning or USB-C version).

### Tier 2 — Lighting (~$30-100)

Even lighting kills harsh shadows on cards and makes slabs look premium.

| Option | Cost AUD | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Neewer 18" ring light** | ~$50-70 | Solid budget option, includes phone holder + tripod usually. |
| **Lume Cube Panel** | ~$120-180 | More compact, better colour accuracy. |
| **Elgato Key Light Air** | ~$250-300 | Premium, app-controlled. Overkill at this stage. |

**Recommendation:** Neewer 18" ring light kit at ~$60. Includes tripod + phone holder. One-purchase setup.

### Tier 3 — Mounts + Surface (~$50-110)

| Item | Cost AUD | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Overhead phone clamp / desk arm** | $30-80 | Search "phone overhead arm desk" — generic Amazon versions work. Smatree or Lume Cube if you want brand. |
| **Ultra Pro Playmat (dark colour)** | $20-30 | Non-glare, frames cards perfectly on camera. Buy from your LGS or Good Games. |
| **Small phone tripod (for face cam)** | $20-40 | Manfrotto PIXI Mini or any decent compact tripod. |

### Tier 4 — Power + Network (~$40-50)

| Item | Cost AUD | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **USB-C / Lightning charger near setup** | $20-30 | Phone never goes below 50% during a stream. |
| **Power bank backup** | $20-30 | Just in case. Anker / Belkin 10000mAh. |
| **WiFi range check** | — | Test your stream location BEFORE going live. Get a mesh extender if signal is weak. |
| **Mobile hotspot as backup** | — | If WiFi dies mid-stream, phone hotspot can keep you online. |

## Phone streaming settings

Before going live on Whatnot:

- [ ] **Camera resolution: 1080p, NOT 4K** — Whatnot streams at 1080p max; 4K just wastes battery + storage
- [ ] **Notifications: Do Not Disturb ON** — no IG/text pings interrupting auctions
- [ ] **Phone plugged into power** — never run a stream on battery alone
- [ ] **WiFi locked in** (or 5G with strong signal)
- [ ] **Whatnot app permissions sorted** — camera, mic, network
- [ ] **Storage cleared** if you're also recording locally — 2hr stream = several GB
- [ ] **Brightness ON max** so you can see chat during the stream
- [ ] **Thermal management** — phones overheat during long streams. Don't stack the phone on a hot surface; keep airflow under it.

## Where to skip spending (for now)

- ❌ DSLR / mirrorless camera — phone is fine, Whatnot doesn't reward higher-fidelity video
- ❌ 4K capture — Whatnot streams at 1080p max
- ❌ Fancy backdrop / branded wall vinyl — printed HOKO sign on the wall is enough
- ❌ Stream deck / control surface — overkill at solo-streamer stage
- ❌ Multi-monitor PC streaming setup — Whatnot is phone-native
- ❌ Lighting kits over $200 — diminishing returns past a single ring light

## The single principle

**Audio > everything else.** A Whatnot stream with a phone camera and clean lav mic audio beats a 4K camera with echo every single time. Buyers stay for the host's voice; the cards are the supporting visual.

If you can only afford one tier from this doc, **buy the RØDE Wireless ME and skip the rest.** Add lights and mounts later.
