# Whatnot Show Prep — HOKO Collectables

Pre-show checklist and run-of-show template. Complete this before every Whatnot live.
Owner reviews and customises per show. Claude fills in the gaps from inputs.

---

---

## Step 1 -- Inventory prep (1 day before)

- [ ] Pull all items to be sold and stage them in order of sell sequence
- [ ] Check Shopify/eBay: are any of these items already listed? Delist or note -- avoid double-selling
- [ ] Set Whatnot minimum prices (default: never below current eBay sold median)
- [ ] Confirm shipping supplies: bubble wrap, rigid mailers, poly bags, tape
- [ ] Weigh or estimate postage for each lot type (sealed box vs singles vs slabs)

## Step 2 -- Stream setup (1 hour before)

- [ ] Camera: phone on tripod, good lighting (window light or ring light), background clean
- [ ] Sound: test audio -- is there echo? Is background noise low?
- [ ] Internet: Ethernet preferred. If WiFi, run speed test (need 10Mbps+ upload)
- [ ] Phone charged to 100% or on charge
- [ ] Items staged and visible behind you or on desk
- [ ] Whatnot app: sign in, check stream title, set scheduled time, confirm payout account
- [ ] Pinned message drafted (see Step 4 below)

## Step 3 -- Stream title and description

Title formula: [Set Name] [Show Type] -- HOKO Collectables -- [Date/Time AEST]

Examples:
- Pokemon Destined Rivals Box Break -- HOKO Collectables -- Sun 8pm AEST
- Japanese Sealed Pack Rip + Slab Showcase -- HOKO Collectables -- Fri 7pm AEST

Description (short, 2-3 sentences):
Collector-run from Melbourne. [What we are opening/selling tonight]. [Price range]. Free tracked AU postage on orders over $100. New customers: use HOKO10 at hokocollectables.com for 10% off.

## Step 4 -- Pinned messages (copy-paste ready)

Welcome pin:
Hey everyone, welcome to HOKO! Collector-run from Melbourne (~20 years hobby experience). We have got [X] lots tonight. Prices start from $[Y]. Free tracked AU postage on orders over $100.

Shop pin:
Browse our full range at hokocollectables.com -- use code HOKO10 for 10% off your first order.

Buyback pin (use if anyone asks about selling):
Looking to sell your collection? We buy factory-sealed TCG + PSA/BGS/CGC graded slabs. DM us or visit hokocollectables.com to find out more.

## Step 5 -- Run of show

Suggested order:
1. Opening (1-2 min): introduce yourself, what is happening tonight, price range
2. Warm-up lot: mid-value item to get bidding going
3. Main lots: highest-value items in the middle of the show (audience peaks mid-stream)
4. Giveaway (if planned): announce early, reveal mid-show to keep viewers watching
5. Flash lots: 2-3 quick lots at the end for urgency
6. Close: plug hokocollectables.com + HOKO10, mention next show

## Step 6 -- Post-show (within 2 hours)

- [ ] Note total sales and number of buyers
- [ ] Pack all sold items same night or first thing next morning
- [ ] Dispatch within 24 hours (same-day dispatch is the HOKO standard)
- [ ] Update Shopify/eBay inventory for anything that sold
- [ ] Post one IG/TikTok recap (best pull or clip from the stream)
- [ ] Note what worked and what did not for next show

---

## Pricing rules

- Never go below current eBay AU sold median for any lot
- Whatnot buyers expect a slight live premium -- it is ok to price 5-10% above eBay
- Giveaways drive watch time -- worth including one mid-value item (~$30-50) per show
- Do not promise prices before the show -- Whatnot auctions start low and climb
