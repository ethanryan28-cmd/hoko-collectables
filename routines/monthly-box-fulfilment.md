## What is the Monthly Box?

A curated monthly TCG subscription box. $59 AUD/month, auto-billed via Shopify Subscriptions.
Each box contains a mix of sealed packs + one featured item (slab, promo, or single).

## Packing standard

Every Monthly Box must:
- Use a rigid-sided shipping box (not poly mailer -- protects the featured item)
- Wrap featured item separately in bubble wrap
- Pack packs flat, secured so they cannot rattle
- Include the HOKO insert card on top (visible when box is opened)
- Seal with tape on all seams
- Label clearly with subscriber name + address

Do NOT use poly mailers for Monthly Box -- a subscriber paying $59/month expects the unboxing experience to match.

## Subscriber management

- Track active subscribers in Shopify Subscriptions dashboard
- New subscriber mid-month: ship their first box within 7 days of signup
- Cancelled subscriber: no refund on current billing cycle (standard Shopify terms) -- confirm this in product description
- Paused subscriber: Shopify handles billing pause; skip their box for that month

Do not accept manual PayPal/cash subscriptions -- all must go through Shopify for billing and tracking.
